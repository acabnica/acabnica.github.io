Pyro

Pyro visions grant their wielders the power of the pyro element (fire).

What is Pyro?

One of the most adaptable components is pyro. Pyro users may utilize their elemental ability to merge with four other elements: electrical, cryo, hydro, and dendro.

Each reaction does extra damage to enemies, either in an AoE burst or by increasing damage over time. All of the elemental reactions for pyro are useful since they can be utilized to easily take on any number of enemies.

Pyro Characters

There are currently 10 Pyro characters. They're Hu tao, Diluc, Klee, Yoimiya, Yanfei, Thoma, Amber, Bennett, Xiangling, Xinyan

Hu Tao

The Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. She took over the business at a rather young age.

Diluc Ragnvindr

Diluc is the current owner of the Dawn Winery and a nobleman of high esteem in Mondstadt society. While a past incident caused him to split ways with the Knights of Favonius, he continues to protect Mondstadt in his own way.

Klee

The daughter of the intrepid and often quite destructive adventurer Alice, Klee takes after her mother in many ways, much to the dismay of the Knights of Favonius whom she has been entrusted to.

Naganohara Yoimiya

She is the daughter of Naganohara Ryuunosuke and the current owner of Naganohara Fireworks. With her colorful fireworks and outgoing personality, Yoimiya is loved by everyone on Narukami Island, as they believe summer is not the same without her.

Yanfei

She is the child of an adeptus father and human mother. After she grew up, her parents left to go on an adventure, placing her in the care of Madame Ping. She resides in Liyue, serving as a legal advisor for its people.

Thoma

Hailing from Mondstadt, he is the housekeeper for the Kamisato Clan, and also a well-known "fixer" in Inazuma.

Amber

As the only remaining Outrider of the Knights of Favonius, she is always ready to help the citizens of Mondstadt — whether it be something simple or perhaps a more challenging task.

Bennett

An orphan discovered by an elderly adventurer as a baby, Bennett was raised in Mondstadt's Adventurers' Guild. He is the one and only member of "Benny's Adventure Team," as everyone else left the team after experiencing the constant misfortune that follows him.

Mao Xiangling

She is the Head Chef of the Wanmin Restaurant, which she co-owns with her father, Chef Mao. Xiangling, as a cook, isn't hesitant to experiment with new dishes or "strange" ingredients... Her foods are sometimes rather distinctive, as a result.

Xinyan

Rock 'n' roll is a relatively unknown musical genre in Liyue, having just been introduced from Fontaine. Xinyan, on the other hand, adores the sound — and she's gradually but steadily building her own fan following as she plays across Liyue Harbor.
